Step 1 · Choose your path

New family or returning?

The forms you need depend on whether your child has been to Oriole before. Both start the same way.

New to Oriole

New families

First time joining the Oriole community — welcome!

  1. Complete Form 1 — Family Registration and Form 2 — Medical & Emergency (submit each separately).
  2. Select your attendance weeks on Form 3 — Week Selection.
  3. Sign the Form 4 — Consent & Waiver.
  4. Submit your child’s immunization records (upload on Form 2 or email later).
Welcome back

Returning families

Your child has attended Oriole school or camp before.

  1. Complete Form 1 — Family Registration.
  2. Select your attendance weeks on Form 3 — Week Selection.
  3. Sign the Form 4 — Consent & Waiver.
Step 2 · Pick your weeks

Choose the weeks that fit your summer.

Book any number of weeks — no full-summer commitment. Holiday weeks are priced for four days. Tap to add up your total, then enter the same weeks on Form 3.

📋 Immunization records required

Summer Camp spaces are not confirmed until immunization records are received. Mail them to the school, or email a copy to heather@oriolenurseryschool.com.

Good to know

Our summer booking policy

Because our weeks are small and fill quickly with returning families, Summer Camp spaces are non-refundable and non-transferable once confirmed. Please also note your child must be able to navigate stairs and playground inclines independently.
