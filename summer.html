The Oriole summer

We bring the classroom outside.

Summer at Oriole is unhurried and joyful. Our educators take everything children love about the school year — play-based discovery, warm small groups, fresh weekly themes — and move it into our spacious, shaded playground. Think outdoor painting, building big structures, water play, music and movement, and plenty of room to just be a kid in the sunshine.
