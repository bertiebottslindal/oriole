The co-operative difference · since 1948

A community that stays close — and consistent.

One of Toronto’s first co-operative nursery schools, Oriole has welcomed generations of Deer Park families since 1948. We’re led by long-tenured educators and guided by a board of directors made up of parents, which keeps our classes small, our fees lower, and our community genuinely involved.

Because our families share the week between school and their own afternoon care, Oriole stays a steady, familiar place — fewer disruptions and more consistency for your child.

Oriole is non-denominational, and families of every background are welcome.
