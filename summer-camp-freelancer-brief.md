# Summer Camp Page — Freelancer Job Brief

## Job post (copy-paste this)

**Title:** Build one page on our existing **classic Wix Editor** site — design & content provided

**Description:**
We need one new page ("Summer Camp") added to our existing Wix site. This is a build-to-spec job — **the design, copy, and images are all provided**, so no design work is needed; we need someone to assemble it natively in the Wix Editor to match the mockup.

We'll provide:
- A finished visual mockup (HTML file you can open in a browser — this is the target look)
- A section-by-section build spec (exact layout, colors, fonts, copy to paste)
- All images, named and ready
- Brand colors + fonts (Fraunces headings, Inter body)

Scope (one page, ~10 sections): hero, info strip, intro, a 6-card "what's included" grid, an 11-item "weekly themes" grid (best as a repeater), a photo gallery, 2 pricing cards, a 3-step "how to register" section, a lead form, and a closing CTA banner. Page must be mobile-responsive.

**Important:** Our site is the **classic Wix Editor** (NOT Wix Studio). Please confirm you work in the classic Editor.

We'll add you as a site collaborator with limited access (no account/password sharing). **Fixed price.** Please share 1–2 examples of Wix pages you've built.

---

## Where to find someone (best for this budget)

1. **Fiverr** — best fit for $50–150. Search **"Wix page design"** or **"Wix expert."** Filter to **Level 2 / Top Rated**, 4.9★+, lots of reviews, "online recently." Message 2–3 with the brief before ordering.
2. **Upwork** — post the job above as **fixed-price ~$100**. Invite freelancers with **90%+ Job Success**, "Wix" in their title, and Wix sites in their portfolio.
3. **Wix Marketplace** (wix.com/marketplace) — official Wix Partners, higher quality but often pricier; worth a quote for comparison.

## How to vet (pick a *good* one)

- ✅ Portfolio shows **real Wix sites** (ask for live links).
- ✅ Confirms they work in **classic Wix Editor**, not only Studio.
- ✅ Many recent reviews, 4.8★+, responds quickly and clearly.
- ✅ Reads the spec and asks sensible questions (a good sign).
- 🚩 Avoid: anyone asking for your **password** (use collaborator invite instead), suspiciously cheap ($5–15) bulk sellers, or vague portfolios.

## Protect yourself

- **Keep payment in-platform** (Fiverr/Upwork escrow) — never pay off-platform up front.
- **Give limited access:** Wix → Site → **Roles & Permissions → Invite people** → add their email as a contributor (e.g., "Website Designer"/"Back Office Manager" role). **Remove access when done.** Never share your login.
- Ask to **review on a preview/unpublished page** before they publish and before final payment.

## Screening questions to send candidates

1. Do you work in the **classic Wix Editor** (not just Wix Studio)? (Yes required.)
2. Share 1–2 **live links** to Wix pages you built.
3. Given a complete mockup + spec + images, what's your **fixed price** and **turnaround** for one ~10-section responsive page?
4. Comfortable building an **11-item repeater** and a **Wix Form**? (Both are in this job.)

## Acceptance criteria (what "done" means)

- [ ] Page matches the mockup section-by-section, brand colors + fonts applied
- [ ] All 10 sections present, correct copy, correct images
- [ ] Weekly-themes section shows all 11 themes (repeater preferred)
- [ ] Lead form works and saves submissions
- [ ] Looks correct on **mobile** (check every section)
- [ ] SEO title + meta description set, URL slug `summer-camp`
- [ ] "Summer Camp" added to the site menu

## Files to send them

- `summer.html` — the visual mockup (the target look)
- `summer-wix-build-spec.md` — section-by-section build instructions
- `summer-wix-ai-section-prompts.md` — optional extra reference
