Dates & policies

Payment schedule, discounts & policies.

Payment schedule

  • A non-refundable application fee of $150 must accompany the completed application form for each prospective new or returning student.
  • An $850 deposit is due four weeks following receipt of a formal acceptance letter from the Registrar.
  • Remaining tuition is due at dates specified by the Registrar. For students enrolled prior to the start of the school year, payment is made by 2 installments via cheques post-dated to August 1 and November 1. For students enrolled after November 1, the application fee and deposit are due prior to the start date, and remaining tuition is due two weeks after the formal acceptance letter.
  • Tuition fees for partial-year applicants will be prorated accordingly.
  • A $50 fee will be charged for any NSF cheques.
  • Full-year applicants have priority enrolment. Partial-year spaces are not guaranteed, and families may be asked to pay for the full year to secure a space.

Sibling discount

  • Siblings enrolled during the same school year (Toddler, Junior or Senior): fees — including the application fee and deposit — are charged at 100% for the first enrolment and at 95% (a 5% discount) for each additional enrolment.

Refund policy

  • The application fee is wholly non-refundable.
  • The deposit is refundable if written notice of withdrawal is received 3 months prior to the student’s start date, and is otherwise typically non-refundable except at the Board’s discretion.
  • Remaining tuition is refundable if Oriole receives written notice of withdrawal no later than June 1st (before the school year begins); after that date refunds are at the Board’s discretion.
  • No partial refunds are granted for extended periods of absence.

Participation

  • Oriole’s “participating program” is core to the school’s history, culture and proposition to families. Under this program, families assist in the classroom via Duty Days, scheduled once bi-weekly per student. Duty Days cover the morning program only (9:00 a.m.–12:00 p.m.) — even if your child stays for the Extended Day.
  • Participating families who are unable to attend their assigned Duty Day may switch with another available participating family, or hire an Assistant at a cost of $100 per day.
  • Non-participating families are not required to assist in the classroom.
  • As a co-operative preschool, every family assists with the overall operations of the school via a Board or non-Board volunteer position, regardless of participating status.
